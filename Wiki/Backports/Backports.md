# Backports

SimplyFellas includes several mods that backport features and content from newer Minecraft versions to version 1.21.1. These backports bring you the latest features, blocks, items, mobs, and mechanics from future versions, allowing you to enjoy content that would otherwise be unavailable in this version.

## About

The SimplyFellas modpack includes comprehensive backport mods that bring content from Minecraft versions 1.22, 1.23, and beyond back to version 1.21.1. These backports are carefully implemented to maintain vanilla balance and aesthetics while adding significant new content to enhance your gameplay experience.

The main backport mods included are:

- **Vanilla Backport**: Provides the core framework and multiple major updates
- **Copper Age Backport**: Adds the Copper Age update content
- **Backported Minecarts**: Brings new minecart mechanics
- **Enderpearl Backport**: Adds new enderpearl mechanics
- **Backported Animal Variants**: Includes new animal variants

Together, these mods add hundreds of new blocks, items, mobs, and mechanics to your game, all while maintaining the vanilla Minecraft feel.

## Backported Updates

### Bundles of Bravery

The Bundles of Bravery update introduces **Bundles** to Minecraft, a revolutionary storage solution that allows you to combine multiple different items into a single inventory slot.

#### Bundle Features

**What are Bundles?**
- Bundles are craftable items that can hold up to 64 item stacks total
- Multiple different item types can be stored in a single bundle
- Perfect for organizing small quantities of various items
- Saves significant inventory space

**Crafting Bundles:**
- **Recipe**: 6 Rabbit Hide + 2 String in a crafting table
- Creates 1 Bundle
- Rabbit Hide can be obtained from rabbits or through trading

**Using Bundles:**
1. **Right-click** with a bundle in hand to open it
2. **Drag items** into the bundle interface
3. **Right-click items** in the bundle to remove them
4. **Shift-right-click** to quickly add items from your inventory

**Bundle Capacity:**
- Each bundle can hold items totaling up to 64 stack units
- For example: 32 cobblestone (1 stack) + 16 iron ingots (1 stack) + 16 coal (1 stack) = 3 stacks total
- Different items count as separate stacks regardless of quantity

**Tips:**
- Use bundles for organizing building materials
- Great for storing small quantities of various resources
- Perfect for exploration - carry multiple item types in one slot
- Combine with shulker boxes for ultimate inventory management
