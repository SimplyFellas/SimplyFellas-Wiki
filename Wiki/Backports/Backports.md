# Backports

SimplyFellas includes several mods that backport features and content from newer Minecraft versions to version 1.21.1. These backports bring you the latest features, blocks, items, mobs, and mechanics from future versions, allowing you to enjoy content that would otherwise be unavailable in this version.

## About

The SimplyFellas modpack includes comprehensive backport mods that bring content from Minecraft versions 1.22, 1.23, and beyond back to version 1.21.1. These backports are carefully implemented to maintain vanilla balance and aesthetics while adding significant new content to enhance your gameplay experience.

The main backport mods included are:

- **Vanilla Backport**: Provides the core framework and multiple major updates
- **Copper Age Backport**: Adds the Copper Age update content
- **Backported Minecarts**: Brings new minecart mechanics
- **Enderpearl Backport**: Adds new enderpearl mechanics
- **Backported Animal Variants**: Includes new animal variants

Together, these mods add hundreds of new blocks, items, mobs, and mechanics to your game, all while maintaining the vanilla Minecraft feel.

---

## Backported Updates

### Bundles of Bravery

The Bundles of Bravery update adds brave badges, a new progression system that rewards players for completing challenges and exploring. This system introduces new achievements and rewards that encourage exploration and adventure.

### The Garden Awakens

The Garden Awakens update brings extensive gardening and nature content. This includes new plants, decorative blocks, and gardening mechanics that add depth to farming and landscaping activities.

### Spring to Life

Spring to Life introduces new life-related content including new mobs, creatures, and nature features. This update adds vibrancy and variety to the natural world.

### Chase the Skies

Chase the Skies adds content related to the sky and air, including new blocks, items, and mechanics focused on vertical exploration and sky-based gameplay.

### Copper Age

The Copper Age update introduces extensive copper-related content. This includes new copper blocks, decorative variants, and copper-based mechanics that expand building and crafting options.

## Backported Mechanics

### Backported Minecart Mechanics

This mod brings new minecart mechanics from newer versions, improving minecart functionality and adding new features to rail-based transportation systems.

### Backported Enderpearl Mechanics

Adds new enderpearl mechanics that enhance teleportation and ender pearl usage, providing new ways to interact with this useful item.

### Backported Animal Variants

Introduces new animal variants that add variety to the mobs you encounter in your world. These variants can be found naturally in the wild and add visual diversity to the game.
