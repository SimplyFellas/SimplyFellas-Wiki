# Food & Cooking

Complete guide to the food system in SimplyFellas - cooking, automation, nutrition, and creating automated food production systems.

---

## Overview

SimplyFellas includes comprehensive food mods that dramatically expand cooking, farming, and culinary options. This guide covers Farmer's Delight, Extra Delight, Butchercraft, Create food integration, and automated food production systems.

---

## Prerequisites

Before starting this guide, you should:
- Have basic survival resources
- Understand [Basic Automation](Basic-Automation) concepts
- Have access to farming materials
- Be ready to set up food production

---

## Key Concepts

### Food Mods Overview

**Core Food Mods:**
- **Farmer's Delight**: Original food mod with extensive recipes
- **Extra Delight**: Expands Farmer's Delight
- **Butchercraft**: Meat processing and butchering
- **Tide**: 60+ new fish types

**Create Integration:**
- **Create: Food**: Automatable food items
- **Create: Confectionary**: Chocolate factories
- **Create: Slice & Dice**: Farmer's Delight automation
- **Create: Central Kitchen**: Extended automation

### Nutrition System

**Hunger and Saturation:**
- Different foods provide different values
- Saturation affects how long you stay full
- Some foods provide special effects
- Balance nutrition for best results
