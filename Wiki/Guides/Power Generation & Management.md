# Power Generation & Management

Complete guide to power systems in SimplyFellas - rotational power, steam engines, diesel generators, electrical systems, and power distribution.

---

## Overview

SimplyFellas features multiple power generation systems through Create and its addons. This guide covers all power types from basic rotational power to advanced diesel and electrical systems, plus power distribution and management strategies.

---

## Prerequisites

Before starting this guide, you should:
- Have mastered [Basic Automation](Basic-Automation)
- Understand Create basics
- Have resources for power systems
- Be ready for advanced power

## Key Concepts

### Power Types

**Rotational Power:**
- Basic Create power
- RPM and stress system
- Waterwheels and windmills
- Foundation for all power

**Steam Power:**
- High-output steam engines

- Scalable systems
- Advanced power generation

**Diesel Power:**
- Industrial diesel generators
- Oil-based fuel system
- Highest power output
- Complex setup

**Electrical Power:**
- Create: Crafts & Additions
- Alternative power system
- Wire-based distribution
- Modern power solution
