# Storage

SimplyFellas includes various mods that enhance your storage capabilities. These mods allow you to store things in drawers, vaults, terminals, backpacks, and more, providing comprehensive storage solutions for all your items.

## About

There are several mods that add new storage capabilities such as Tom's Storage with an AE2-like storage network except made out of wood. There's also Functional Storage which adds various drawers to store items, including an ender drawer. Combined with sophisticated backpacks, advanced shulker boxes, and carry-on functionality, SimplyFellas provides a complete storage ecosystem.

## Storage Networks

### Tom's Simple Storage Mod

This mod adds simple AE2-like storage terminals. The wireless terminals can be accessed at further distances according to beacon strength, with the best being powered by a level 4 beacon nearby. Create a centralized storage network that allows you to access all your items from anywhere!

## Drawer Storage

### Functional Storage

Adds various drawers as well as drawer upgrades and other useful capabilities. Drawers provide efficient bulk storage for items, with visual displays showing item counts and easy access. Perfect for organizing your resources!

<img src="Wiki/Storage/FunctionalStorage.png" alt="Functional Storage" class="inline-image">

---

## Portable Storage

### Sophisticated Backpacks

This mod adds various types of backpacks, as well as backpack upgrades. These are super useful for mining or holding items you want to access easily if you don't have a wireless terminal yet. These can also be used with magnet upgrades while placed down for mob farms. Upgrade your backpacks with filters, auto-pickup, and more!

<img src="Wiki/Storage/SophisticatedBackpacks.png" alt="Sophisticated Backpacks" class="inline-image">

### Sophisticated Backpacks Create Integration

This mod allows you to use sophisticated backpacks with various Create contraptions. Integrate your backpacks into your automation systems!

### Advanced Shulkerboxes

This mod allows you to right-click and instantly open a shulker box. You can crouch and right-click to place it normally. Makes shulker boxes much more convenient to use!

---

## Item Management

### Carry On

This mod allows you to pick up any storage block (such as chests) by crouching and right-clicking with both empty hands to pick it up. This also works on any mob and also your friends! Perfect for moving chests and storage around your base.

<img src="Wiki/Storage/CarryOn.png" alt="Carry On" class="inline-image">

### Carry On Extend

This mod allows you to crouch and press Q to throw anything you're holding. You can also crouch and scroll to adjust the power. Adds throwing functionality to the Carry On system.

### Trash Cans

Adds trash cans which can be used to void items, liquids, and energy! Perfect for cleaning up unwanted items and managing factory waste streams.

<img src="Wiki/Storage/TrashCans.png" alt="Trash Cans" class="inline-image">

---

## Create Integration

### Create Stuff and Additions Tank Fix

This mod allows you to use the Create tanks with the Sophisticated Backpacks fluid upgrades. Integrate Create's fluid systems with your backpack storage!

---

## Storage Strategy

With Tom's Simple Storage for centralized access, Functional Storage drawers for bulk items, Sophisticated Backpacks for portable storage, and Advanced Shulkerboxes for quick access, you have all the storage solutions you need for any situation!
